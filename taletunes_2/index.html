taletunes
Elevate your game's atmosphere with taletunes, a tool for seamlessly blending ambient soundscapes and music to match every moment. Set the perfect tone for every scene. Dynamic, immersive sound-design at your fingertips.

The Pencil-Icon toggles Edit-Mode so you can add a group and youtube-urls to it. Activate or deactivate a group by clicking it. Hit the [add-Tune] to add a youtube-url to the group. Clicking the apearing image lets you play the audio and also increase or decrease the audio volume. Clicking low sets the target Volume, clicking left or right sets the Fading-Speed from immediate to a 30s transition.
